Unfortunately however, this lead him down a road where all he can think about is winning. Not a day passes without him wanting to play an online match and try to beat his opponents. Every win was an ego boost and every loss was a sorrow defeat. You couldn’t help but feel concerned for the guy, but if that’s what he wants to dedicate his life towards, you thought: “be my guest”.
